## Design Context

### Users
RapidWebStudio is used by local business owners in India who need a fast, premium-looking website that works well on mobile and converts casual visitors into calls, WhatsApp chats, and bookings. The demo pages are shown to prospects as proof of quality across different business categories.

### Brand Personality
Fast, polished, conversion-focused. The work should feel credible enough for service businesses, distinct enough to stand out from low-effort templates, and practical enough to launch quickly.

### Aesthetic Direction
Each demo should have its own visual identity instead of sharing one house style. All demos should feel premium, mobile-first, and intentionally designed, while keeping content clear for local-search visitors who usually want pricing, trust signals, directions, and a fast way to contact the business.

### Design Principles
1. Put the primary conversion action above the fold and repeat it in mobile-friendly ways.
2. Match the visual tone to the business category instead of reusing generic landing-page patterns.
3. Use strong local trust signals: ratings, timings, credentials, packages, and real-world context.
4. Keep interactions lightweight, touch-friendly, and fast in plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
5. Make each demo feel ready to sell to a real client, not just look good in a screenshot.
