FAQ

Questions patients ask before they tap book.

Do you take same-day appointments?

Yes. Most patients book same-day via phone or WhatsApp, especially for fever, seasonal illness, and follow-up visits.

What is the consultation fee?

General consultation starts at Rs.700. Preventive screening packages and repeat review bundles are shared before confirmation.

Can I book for a child or elderly parent?

Yes. Mention the age and symptoms while booking so reception can suggest the most suitable doctor and slot.

Do you share digital prescriptions?

Yes. Prescriptions and follow-up instructions are shared digitally for easier repeat consultation and medicine ordering.
