Our Story

Crafted With
Passion & Purpose

What started as a tiny 200 sq ft corner shop has grown into one of Pune's most loved neighbourhood cafés. We source single-origin beans directly from Coorg and Chikmagalur, roast them in-house, and brew every cup to perfection.

Our kitchen uses locally sourced, seasonal ingredients. Every dish is prepared fresh — no frozen shortcuts, no artificial flavours.

Single-Origin Beans Directly sourced from Indian estates
Freshly Baked Daily Croissants, muffins & sourdough from our oven
Vegan & Healthy Options Plant-based dishes & gluten-free choices
