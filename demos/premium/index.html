Our Story

It started with a terrible office coffee.

In 2019, Vikram made a decision that his friends called "dramatic" and his parents called "concerning" — he quit a perfectly good IT job because the office Nescafe was genuinely terrible. Not "could be better" terrible. More like "who approved this" terrible.

Six months, one trip to Coorg's coffee estates, and several questionable financial decisions later, Brew & Bite opened its doors on FC Road. The mission was simple: serve coffee that doesn't make people sad, and pair it with food that people actually want to eat before noon.

"If the coffee's bad, everything else doesn't matter."

— Vikram, Founder (still dramatic)
Beans from Coorg Single-origin, roasted weekly in small batches right here in Pune.
Baked before dawn Our baker Suresh starts at 4 AM. Every pastry, every loaf — made in-house.
Vegan-friendly menu Oat milk is default, not an upcharge. Plant-based options across the board.
