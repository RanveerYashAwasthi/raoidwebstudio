Our Story

Brewed with Passion,
Served with Love

What started as a small coffee cart in 2021 has grown into Koramangala's favorite neighborhood café. Every cup we serve is a blend of carefully sourced beans, skilled baristas, and a genuine love for great coffee.

We believe a café isn't just about the coffee — it's about the experience. From the aroma that greets you at the door to the last sip of your latte, we craft moments worth savoring.
